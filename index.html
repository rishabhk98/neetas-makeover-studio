Welcome to Neeta's Makeover Studio

Nestled in the heart of Gomti Nagar, Lucknow, Neeta's Makeover Studio is your go-to destination for premium beauty and grooming services. Under the expert guidance of Neeta Verma, we have been transforming looks and boosting confidence for years.

From everyday hair care and skin treatments to stunning bridal transformations, we combine the latest beauty techniques with genuine care for each client. Our goal is simple — help you walk out feeling more beautiful than when you walked in.
